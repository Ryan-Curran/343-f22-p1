Thursday, November 10th, 2022

Well Happy Thursday everyone. I have been out for a couple of days doing that stuff you put off all year long and for a good reason, I hate doing it. Well, we had a good stretch of good weather and as you know it's been windy and cool as of late and we have a storm coming. The fishing the last couple of days has been a little better we have seen some drum off the piers and some speckled and grey trout with mullet this morning. South of Oregon Inlet you will still get some big drum, bluefish, mullet, and a lot of sharks down toward the point. Trout fishing is still good to the inside but they have been scattered at times. We do have rough conditions out today and until the storm passes with a water temp of 62 today. For now, that's all the news we have as we near the end the yet another season.
