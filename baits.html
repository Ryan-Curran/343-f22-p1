Swimbait

Swimbaits (soft plastics) require a jighead to use, and are the go to artifical lure for many anglers. Throw these out and let them sink for 2-3 seconds, then use a medium retrieval on the way in.

Spoon

Spoons are great for catching drum. You'll want to avoid grassy areas as their treble hook easily picks up unecessary gunk. Throw them out and bring them in with a slow to medirum retrieval.

Live bait

If you're wondering whether to use live bait or artificials, live bait always wins. Hook up a croaker with a circle hook, sit back, and wait for a bite.
